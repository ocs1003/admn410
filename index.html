About Us

History

Started in 1970 by Jonathan Tate, Durham House of Pets has grown to become a widespread pet store in the United States. It has thrived off of its family-feel and helpful staff. Originally, DHP sold only dog supplies, but has since grown to incorporate both cats and snakes. It was not until 1984 that Tate decided to begin carrying cat supplies in his Durham location. The snake section of the store came into play nearly 7 years later. In 2008, Jonathan Tate and his two sons, Jackson and Garrett, decided to make their operation a large scale pet supply store rather than a local one location shop. The Tates moved their headquarters to an office in Boston, and began looking at locations to open other retail stores in. In 2011 the three men opened up two additional locations: Charleston, SC, and Burlington, VT.

Why use us?

What sets us apart from the rest? At Durham House of Pets, each and every employee shares the same love for pets. We pride ourselves on our dedication to going above and beyond for every family that comes into one of our stores because, after all, pets are part of your family! Not only do we offer supplies and food for your dogs, cats, and snakes, but we also offer dog wash stations, nail care, and basic training courses. It is our mission to help you help your pets!
