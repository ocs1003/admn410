Policies

Financing Policy

Durham House of Pets offers financing for all of its services. These services include cat and dog training, nail clipping, bathing, and grooming. These services are available for single visits, but are also offered as a financed subscription. Customers can purchase various annual memberships to include the specific services they wish to get for their pet. Each membership can be paid in full or be broken into monthly payments in order to spread out the costs. Buying an annual membership can save you hundreds each year and breaking down the annual cost does not impact the overall total cost of the membership!

Delivery Policy

Orders must be placed via phone, E-mail, or in person. At this time, our website does not support online ordering. If we do not have an item you are looking for, we will have it shipped either to one of our locations or directly to your house for no extra charge.

We ship anywhere in the United States for free. We also ship to Canada for a small fee.

Shipping takes 5-7 days after the order has been processed. Orders will ship through UPS, FedEx, or USPS depending on its final destination. Tracking information will be provided by the mail carrier.

Return Policy

Customer satisfaction is a major focus here at Durham House of Pets. We want each and every customer and pet to love their new products. If you are not pleased with your purchase for any reason, DHP accepts returns with a receipt - no questions asked! If you do not have a receipt we are able to look up the purchase in our system using your phone number or email address. Customers will be issued a full refund or a replacement product depending on the situation.
